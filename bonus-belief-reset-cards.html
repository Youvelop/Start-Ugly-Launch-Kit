10 cards. 10 beliefs. 10 reframes that actually hold up.

These are not affirmations. Affirmations tell you to feel something different. These tell you to think something different — which is harder and more useful.

The 10 Cards

Read One.
Do The Action.

Each card has two sides. The belief is what you currently tell yourself. The reframe is the more accurate version of reality. Read both. Do the micro action before moving to the next card.

How To Use These

Three Ways
To Use This.

01
As a Buyer
One card per day for 10 days. Do the micro action before moving to the next one. Reading without acting is just entertainment.
02
As Content
Post one card per day for 10 days on stories or Threads. Add a poll or "which one hits hardest" prompt. Engagement will be higher than almost anything else you post.
03
As a Reference
The beliefs come back at different stages of building. When they do, find the relevant card and read it again. That's what it's here for.
Design Instructions

The Canva
Brief.

When you build these in Canva, keep it simple. Two contrasting treatments — one for Side A (the belief), one for Side B (the reframe). Consistent across all 10 cards so they feel like a set.

Specifications
Canvas size1080 × 1920px — Instagram story format
Cards to build10 cards × 2 sides = 20 slides total
ColorsYour brand colors — one dark treatment for Side A, one light for Side B
FontOne display font for belief text, one readable body font for reframe
StyleClean. Minimal. High contrast. No gradients or busy backgrounds.
